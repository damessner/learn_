title: Reported Speech Practice
description: Practice reporting statements and questions (Units 3 & 4).
type: gap-fill
---
Change the sentences from Direct Speech into Reported Speech:

1. Direct: "I am ready for the job interview," she said.
Reported: She said that she [was|is|be] ready for the job interview.

2. Direct: "We will make an emergency landing," the pilot told the passengers.
Reported: The pilot told the passengers that they [would make|will make|make] an emergency landing.

3. Direct: "Where do you want to work?" the career advisor asked.
Reported: The career advisor asked me where I [wanted|want|to want] to work.

4. Direct: "Did you inspect the evidence?" the officer asked the witness.
Reported: The officer asked the witness if they [had inspected|inspected|inspect] the evidence.

5. Direct: "I don't like my working hours," Philip said to me.
Reported: Philip told me that he [didn't like|doesn't like|not like] his working hours.
